1. Run GUI and Maximize the app for best experience
2. Cick on Load File(10 seconds) and browse to the .xlsx Candidates file.
3. After Loading file click on Divide and Scrutiny Shortlist Candidates( 1:30-4 minutes)
4. Click on the desired button to access respective Scrutiny Shortlisted Candidates.
5. When ready, click on Further Shortlist Candidates button
6. Make changes to cut-offs if required and Click on Go (1-3 minutes)
7. Click on the desired button to access respective Final Shortlisted Candidates.
